---
title: Exercise Metrics
description: Metrics to track for exercise at a micro and macro level
---

## Micro

- Type (stability, strength, aerobic performance, anaerobic output, rucking, walking, NEAT)
- Duration
- Calories burned
- Average heart rate
- Maximum heart rate

## Macro

- Calories burned
- Inactivity time
- Exercise miniutes
- Stand hours
- Steps
- Flights climbed
- Ideally: MET hours
