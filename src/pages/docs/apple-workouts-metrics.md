## Metrics the EARLY platform collects from Apple Workouts

### Workouts

- Date
- Type (stability, strength, aerobic performance, anaerobic output, rucking, walking, NEAT)
- Duration
- Active Energy (kcal)
- Maximum heart rate (bpm)
- Average heart rate (bpm)
